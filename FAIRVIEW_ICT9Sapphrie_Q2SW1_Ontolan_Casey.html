DOG BREEDS

Image Breed Description
German Shepherd The medium- to large-sized German working dog breed known as the German Shepherd or Alsatian. Max von Stephanitz used a variety of traditional German herding dogs starting in 1899 to create the breed. It was originally bred to herd sheep as a herding dog.
Siberian Husky A medium-sized working sled dog breed is the Siberian Husky. The breed is genetically related to the Spitz. It is smaller than the similarly shaped Alaskan Malamute and is easily distinguished by its heavily furred double coat, upright triangular ears, and unusual markings.
Pembroke Welsh Corgi This breed is extremely affectionate, devoted, and loves spending time with its family. Additionally, Corgis must spend the majority of their time with their humans to prevent destructive habits, making them ideal for households with kids or stay-at-home mothers.
